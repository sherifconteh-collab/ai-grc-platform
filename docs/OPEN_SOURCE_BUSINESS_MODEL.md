## ğŸ’¬ FAQs

**Q: Won't companies just use the free version?**  
A: Most will! That's okay. 10-20% convert to paid. With open source, you get 10x more users trying it.

**Q: What if someone forks my code and competes?**  
A: They can, but you have the advantage: community, expertise, first-mover, brand. GitLab has competitors, still worth $8B.

**Q: Can I change the license later?**  
A: Technically yes, but it's a bad look. HashiCorp tried this, community revolted. Stick with MIT.

**Q: Should I take venture funding?**  
A: Optional. You can build a $5-10M/year business bootstrapped. Take funding if you want to grow faster or need capital for sales team.

**Q: How long until I make money?**  
A: First dollar: 1-3 months. Livable income: 6-12 months. $100k+/year: 12-24 months. Depends on your network and hustle.
